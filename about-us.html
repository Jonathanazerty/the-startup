Our story

Culture cuisine was born on the 3th of June 2021 by three passionate people who wanted to express their
passion for food, diversity and helping people through sharing the diversity in food with the world.
Thanks to our passion for discovering new cultures, therefore also new food and meeting new people.
We realised that we had to find a way to combine those elements together and start a company together.
Guess what? We started our journey with Culture Cuisine.

The beauty of the diversity in people which brings diversity in the food is also an important thrive for us as a company.
Culture cuisine is focused on bringing people together and let them discover the world through amazing food.

Picture this : Notice all the colors in our food, and make a mental note of the divers nutrients and divers nourishment the food contains.
and those stir a meaningful blend of thoughts, feelings and emotions.
Therefore the essence of our lives is to live through our passion and values, which are aligned.
Above all else, we want to inspire others to chase their dream and passion and to let no one stop them from getting there.

Let's enjoy the food journey together !
